# 📄 International Conference

## Conference
**ICIRSEM 2026 – International Conference on Integrated Research in Science, Engineering and Management**

## Paper Title
**Automated Drug Dispenser for Efficient Hospital Medication Management**

## Abstract
This research presents an **Automated Drug Dispenser** designed to improve medication management in hospitals through IoT and automation technologies. The system ensures accurate medicine dispensing based on patient prescriptions, reducing human errors and enhancing operational efficiency. RFID technology is used for patient identification, while GSM enables real-time notifications. The solution provides secure, timely, and automated medication delivery, improving patient safety and streamlining healthcare workflows.

## Technologies
- Arduino UNO
- ESP32
- RFID (MFRC522)
- GSM Module (SIM800L)
- Servo Motor
- LCD Display
- Blynk IoT
- Embedded C
- Arduino IDE
